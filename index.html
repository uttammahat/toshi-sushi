Welcome To Toshi Sushi

We are a brand new Japanese / Asian restaurant. You can come to us for both sushi and Asian dishes. The menu varies from Dim-Sums, gyoza, tempura and teriyaki to curry and wok dishes. In addition, there is also an extensive choice of different sushi dishes. Delivery as well as collection is possible.
